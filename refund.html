Refund Policy

Last Updated: January 31, 2026

Overview

At Bint-e-Samin Soaps, we are committed to providing you with high-quality handmade natural soaps. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a fair and straightforward refund policy.

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds are available under the following circumstances:

  • Products received are damaged during shipping
  • Products received are not as described on our website
  • Products received are defective or have manufacturing issues
  • Wrong items shipped due to our error

Non-Eligible Situations

Due to the nature of our handmade natural soaps, we cannot offer refunds for:

  • Products that have been used or opened
  • Products that have been misused or stored improperly
  • Products that have been used by individuals with known allergies to ingredients
  • Change of mind or dissatisfaction with scent/color preferences
  • Products past the return period (more than 7 days after delivery)

Return Period

You have 7 calendar days after receiving your order to request a refund. After 7 days, we can't offer you a refund or exchange.

Return Process

To initiate a return, please contact us via WhatsApp at +92 334 3601488 or email us at info@pureessencesoaps.com with the following information:

  • Order number
  • Reason for return
  • Photos of the damaged/defective product (if applicable)
  • Preferred resolution (refund or replacement)

Refund Processing

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you an email to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If approved, your refund will be processed, and a credit will automatically be applied to your original payment method within 5-10 business days.

Refund Amounts

Refunds will be issued for:

  • Full product cost for damaged/defective items
  • Shipping costs if the damage was due to our packaging
  • Partial refunds may be considered in special circumstances

Replacement Policy

If you prefer a replacement instead of a refund, we can send you a new product at no additional cost. Please note:

  • Replacement items will be shipped within 3-5 business days
  • You may need to return the original item depending on the situation
  • Same-day shipping may be available for urgent cases

Damaged Shipments

If your order arrives damaged:

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  • Provide photos of the damage
  • Keep all original packaging for inspection if requested
  • We will arrange for pickup or provide return instructions

Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or product defect. If you receive a refund, the cost of return shipping will be deducted from your refund.

Special Circumstances

In exceptional cases, we may make exceptions to our policy. Please contact us to discuss your specific situation. We reserve the right to modify this policy at any time.

Quality Assurance

We take pride in our products and implement strict quality control measures. Every soap is carefully inspected before shipping to ensure it meets our high standards.

Contact Information

If you have questions about our Refund Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: info@pureessencesoaps.com
  • WhatsApp: +92 334 3601488
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours during business days

Additional Notes

Please note that our soaps are handmade in small batches, so slight variations in appearance are normal and add to the uniqueness of each bar. This does not constitute a defect and is not eligible for refund.
